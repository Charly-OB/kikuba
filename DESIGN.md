# Kikuba Design System

Kikuba is a warm, editorial and operational digital agency brand. The interface should feel calm, premium, human and useful for non-technical business owners.

## Brand Principles

- First understand, then build.
- The logo is final and must not be redrawn, recolored, simplified or modified.
- The site should sell clarity, not complexity.
- Kikuba listens to the people who run the business and the people who work inside the operation.
- Avoid dark hacker, cyberpunk, crypto, generic SaaS blue, loud startup gradients and fake agency scale.

## Palette

- Deep graphite: `#25302B` for primary text, buttons and core UI.
- Sage green: `#A9B99B` for calm accents and secondary UI.
- Light sand: `#EFE8D7` for main background.
- Toasted sand: `#D8C7A6` for warm highlights and dividers.
- Warm paper: `#FFFDF8` for cards and section surfaces.
- Dark text: `#151A17` for readable body copy.

## Typography

- Headings: Fraunces or Georgia-style editorial serif.
- Body/UI: Inter or a clean humanist sans.
- Use large type only for hero and major section headings.
- Keep letter spacing moderate. The `KIKUBA` wordmark in UI may use tracking, but navigation and body text must stay readable.

## Layout

- Use generous whitespace, simple grids and clear section rhythm.
- Header should show only the logo symbol plus `KIKUBA`.
- The slogan `Observamos - Analizamos - Construimos` may appear in footer or brand system contexts, not as extra header clutter.
- Do not use NFC, sticker, QR or hidden-campaign visuals on the main landing page.
- The node language is a subtle background/detail system, not the main navigation.

## Components

- Buttons are rectangular with minimal radius, clear contrast and concise labels.
- Cards should be calm, bordered, warm and lightweight.
- Icons use `lucide-react` as the default set.
- Do not mix icon families unless a future direction explicitly approves it.
- Use `Detras de Kikuba` instead of `Staff` for the founder/brand-origin section.

## Motion

- Use Motion sparingly.
- Approved patterns: `FadeUp`, `StaggerCards`, `SoftHover`, `NodePulse`.
- Animate hero entrance, card reveal, button hover and subtle node pulse only.
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`.
- Do not animate the logo aggressively. No spinning, glitching, bouncing or letter-by-letter effects.

## Copy

- Main message: `Primero entendemos. Luego construimos.`
- Keep copy commercial and clear for non-technical business owners.
- Explain outcomes: fewer lost messages, clearer inventory, better reports, easier follow-up, better decisions.
- Keep the staff-listening idea focused on the client's team, not as a claim that Kikuba has a large internal team.

## Do / Don't

Do:
- Use warm paper textures, soft shadows, subtle lines and operational language.
- Make the page feel trustworthy, grounded and practical.
- Keep WhatsApp CTA visible and natural.

Don't:
- Add NFC/sticker visuals to the main landing.
- Make the site feel like a hacker brand, crypto project or generic software template.
- Invent a large team, case studies or credentials that do not exist yet.
- Modify the logo.
