# Kikuba Motion And Video Guide

This guide documents future visual tools without adding them as website dependencies.

## Jitter

Use Jitter for social content, not the main landing page.

Best uses:
- Reels and stories introducing Kikuba.
- Short videos with the phrase `Primero entendemos. Luego construimos.`
- Method explainers: `Observamos`, `Analizamos`, `Construimos`.
- Service videos about WhatsApp order flow, inventory, reports and operational clarity.

Rules:
- Use the Kikuba palette.
- Keep motion calm and editorial.
- Avoid neon, glitch, cyberpunk and fast noisy transitions.

## SVGator

Use SVGator only after creating a faithful SVG version of the final logo.

Best uses:
- Logo reveal for video intros/outros.
- Subtle node-line drawing.
- Small animated brand marks for presentations.

Rules:
- Do not redraw or reinterpret the logo.
- Do not change logo colors.
- Keep animation short, precise and premium.

## Anime.js

Anime.js is optional and experimental for future node systems.

Best uses:
- Advanced interactive node maps.
- SVG line drawing when Motion is not enough.
- Campaign microsites, not the main v1 landing.

Rules:
- Do not add Anime.js to the landing unless there is a clear technical need.
- Keep the node system secondary and accessible.
- Never make animation necessary to understand the content.
